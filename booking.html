Tickets, Tours, and Educational Extras

enhance your visit

The admission is free for everyone, but if you want to enhance your visit you can take a look at some of the extras that we offer. The price is per person.

guided tours - 70,-

Join a 30-60 minute guided tour led by our enthusiastic and knowledgeable staff. Learn more, ask questions, and explore the exhibits in depth.

Workshop packages - 80,-

Book a hands-on science workshop tailored to your group's age level. Perfect for sparking curiosity and reinforcing classroom learning.

lunch room reservations - 100,-

Reserve a dedicated lunch space for your group to relax and recharge—especially useful for school field trips and large parties.

birthday packages - 120,-

Celebrate with science! Combine museum access with party space, fun activities, and a special science-themed surprise.
