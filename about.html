about us

Your Local Hub for Science, Learning, and Wonder

who are we?

We are a local museum dedicated to making science engaging, accessible, and fun for children, families, and schools. Through interactive exhibits, educational programs, and special events, we aim to spark interest in science, technology, and the world around us.

Whether you're a teacher planning a school visit, a parent looking for a fun and meaningful day out, or a young explorer ready to dive into discovery—you're welcome here.

  • Children (Under 16): FREE
  • Adult: 120 NOK
  • Student / Senior: 90 NOK
  • Family ticket (2+2): 250 NOK
  • School Groups (per student): 20 NOK
  • Groups (10+ people): 15% Discount

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get:

  • Salads
  • Wraps
  • Baguettes
  • Soft drinks
  • Coffee
  • Wheelchair ramps
  • Audio guides
  • Braille displays
  • Restrooms with support rails
  • Sign language interpreted tours available on request

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you've left the museum.
