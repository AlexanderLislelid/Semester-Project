space zone

Explore the wonders of the cosmos

the sky above us

Our fantastic exhibition, 'The Sky Above Us', explores the night sky and what we can see and know about the universe around us. We'll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen and learn a bit about the early navigators who used the stars to travel by.

vr spacewalk

Ever wondered what it's like to float through space?

Put on your VR headset and take a breathtaking journey outside the International Space Station. Our VR Spacewalk lets you step into the boots of a real astronaut, hovering high above Earth with nothing but the stars around you.

This immersive experience is perfect for curious minds of all ages. Whether you1re dreaming of becoming an astronaut or just want to see Earth from a whole new perspective, this is your chance to explore the final frontier—no rocket required!
