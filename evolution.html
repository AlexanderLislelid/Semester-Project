Evolution

The story of us

Stomp back in time

Get ready for a roaring good time as you travel millions of years into the past! At our Evolution Exhibit, you'll come face to face with towering dinosaurs, furry mammoths, and curious creatures that no longer walk the Earth - like the clumsy Dodo and the mighty saber-toothed cat.

This is no ordinary museum trip - here, you can explore real fossils, touch ancient bones, and even test your roar against a T. rex! Walk through prehistoric jungles, peek into an ice age, and see how life has changed over time - from tiny bugs to giant beasts, and all the way to… us!
